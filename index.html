Botanica Jelitkowo - Brand Identity
We made brand identity for Botanica Jelitkowo from Gdańsk. Botanica Jelitkowo is a cozy estate composing with residential buildings of the seaside district of Gdańsk. It combines an excellent location close to nature: sea, beach, numerous parks and a water reservoir with the proximity of the city's infrastructure.

The architectural design

The architectural design was created based on the ideas of the garden city, with carefully composed vegetation harmonizing with the surroundings. This was the way to create brand identity. We made custom typography of the name and dynamic additional brand element.
